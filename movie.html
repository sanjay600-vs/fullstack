Top 5 Movies of All Time

1. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A story of hope and friendship inside a prison.

2. The Godfather (1972)

A crime saga focusing on the powerful Italian-American mafia family.

3. The Dark Knight (2008)

Batman faces his greatest challenge: The Joker.

4. Pulp Fiction (1994)

A stylish and nonlinear crime story with unforgettable dialogue.

5. Forrest Gump (1994)

The life journey of a kind-hearted man who witnesses major historical events.
